What is n8n?

n8n, essentially, is a more powerful version of Zapier. It is a workflow automation tool that lets you connect various apps and services with custom logic on a drag-and-drop canvas. You can create automated workflows for tasks like data syncing, notifications, integrations, and much more.

My Experience with n8n
